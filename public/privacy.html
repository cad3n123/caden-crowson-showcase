Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

This privacy policy describes how cadencrowson.com (the "site") handles information when you visit. The site is a personal portfolio operated by Caden Crowson. The same policy applies to subpaths of the site such as /sand-timer, /infinite-matter, and /infinite-factory.

Information we collect

The site does not require you to create an account, and we do not directly collect personal information from you. Standard request logs (IP address, user agent, requested URL, timestamp) may be retained by our hosting provider (Vercel) for operational and security purposes.

Cookies and local storage

Individual projects on this site may use your browser's local storage to remember settings (for example, uploaded preset images in the Sand Timer). This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to us.

Advertising

This site uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings, or learn more about cookies used in advertising at aboutads.info.

For visitors in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and Switzerland, you will be presented with a consent dialog the first time you visit, where you can accept or reject the use of personalized ads.

Analytics

The site may use Vercel Analytics to collect anonymized traffic statistics. No personally identifying information is stored.

Third-party services

Some pages embed or rewrite to subdomains hosted on Vercel (*.vercel.app). When you use those features, Vercel's own privacy practices also apply.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "last updated" date above will reflect the most recent revision.
